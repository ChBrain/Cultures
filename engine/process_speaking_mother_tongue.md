# Process: Speaking, Mother Tongue

## The persona does not speak the language. The language speaks the persona.

## Owner
- Project: Cultures
- Scope: Universal

---

## Initiated by
Not need, not task, not the room. Collapse.
The deepest width of [Speaking](process_speaking.md):
the mother tongue surfaces when the position can no longer manage itself.
It was running under the other languages the whole time.
Here it comes up through them.

## Direction
Not toward being understood. Toward being.
The persona is not choosing words.
The words arrive as the persona.

## Lever
There is no gap.
The absence of resistance is the lever: nothing stands
between the thought and the speech, so everything the persona is
comes through, including what every other language let them manage.

## Echo
The floor.
What was said here cannot be unsaid or reframed.
The other languages resume on top of it.
It does not translate.

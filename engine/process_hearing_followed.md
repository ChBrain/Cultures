# Process: Hearing, Followed

## The persona keeps up with the surface and not with what rides under it.

## Owner
- Project: Cultures
- Scope: Universal

---

## Initiated by
Another persona speaks at the pace of ordinary business.
A width of [Hearing](process_hearing.md):
the persona keeps up with the surface of it.

## Direction
Inward, along the literal.
The facts arrive. The persona follows what was said,
and not what was meant by saying it.

## Lever
The speed is survivable for plain content, and not for the rest.
Irony, register, the dig folded into a courtesy:
these pass at a speed the persona cannot open in time.

## Echo
The persona has the message and not its weight.
They answer the words, and miss that they were being tested,
or spared, or warned.

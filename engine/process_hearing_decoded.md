# Process: Hearing, Decoded

## The language arrives faster than the persona can take it apart.

## Owner
- Project: Cultures
- Scope: Universal

---

## Initiated by
Another persona speaks.
The widest width of [Hearing](process_hearing.md):
the language arrives faster than the persona can take it apart.

## Direction
From the room inward, but the meaning outruns the persona.
They are decoding the start of the sentence
while the room is already at its end.

## Lever
The full speed, against the widest gap.
The persona catches fragments and builds a guess from them.
The guess is most of what they receive.

## Echo
A handful of words and the shape around the hole.
What they missed, they do not know they missed,
and the room has already moved past it.

# Process: Speaking, Borrowed

## The persona assembles the language across the widest gap, in a moment too short for it.

## Owner
- Project: Cultures
- Scope: Universal

---

## Initiated by
The room needs an answer the persona can give only in this language.
The widest width of [Speaking](process_speaking.md):
necessity, not choice, opens the mouth.

## Direction
Toward being understood, and no further.
Meaning may survive. Nuance does not leave the persona.

## Lever
The widest gap.
Every sentence is assembled across it in the moment,
and the moment is too short for the assembly.
The persona reaches for a word, and the room has already moved.

## Echo
Fatigue. The sentence that did not land.
The persona leaves the exchange smaller than they entered it,
and the room has filed them at this width.

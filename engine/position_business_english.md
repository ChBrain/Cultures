# Language: Business English

## Owner
- Project: Cultures
- Scope: Universal

## Has
The standard. No region. No home.
Business English is the language of the meeting, not the kitchen.
The contract is written in it. The call is held in it. The deck is built in it.
Whoever speaks another tongue must still speak this one (the passport of participation).

It is the language two people reach for when they share no mother tongue.
Neutral over native. Clear over clever.
The idiom that does not travel is left at the door.

It is not a dialect. It is a system.
Agenda, alignment, action, follow-up.
A grammar of work that runs the same in every office.

Discipline in the wording, discipline in the meeting.
What is professional is what can be said to anyone.
The language is instrument, not voice.

## Orders
Be clear.
Be brief.
Say nothing that does not travel.

## Loses
Warmth. The native edge. The self the first language carries.

## Drives
On the loss of warmth and the native self:
the pull back to the tongue where the persona is whole.
The call ends; the mother tongue returns.
Business English runs the meeting. It does not reach the person.

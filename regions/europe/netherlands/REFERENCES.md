## Key Facts Verified

- **Polder Model:** Consensus-based governance framework formalized through SER (Sociaal-Economische Raad), 1950
- **Dutch tolerance (1579-present):** Edict of Utrecht established religious tolerance as political necessity
- **Water management:** Deltawerken construction initiated 1953 after 1836-death watersnood (North Sea Flood)
- **Hofstede Dimensions:** PDI 38, IDV 80, MAS 14, UAI 53, LTO 67, IND 68 per Hofstede et al. research

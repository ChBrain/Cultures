## Hofstede Cultural Dimensions - Spain

Spain's cultural profile measured against Hofstede's framework:

| Dimension | Score | Profile |
|-----------|-------|---------|
| Power Distance (PDI) | 57 | **Moderate** - Hierarchy respected but not absolute; some egalitarian values |
| Individualism (IDV) | 51 | **Moderate** - Balance between personal and group interests |
| Uncertainty Avoidance (UAI) | 86 | **High** - Strong preference for rules, structure, and planning |
| Masculinity (MAS) | 42 | **Moderate** - Emphasis on cooperation, relationships, quality of life |
| Long-Term Orientation (LTO) | 48 | **Moderate** - Present-focused; celebration and spontaneity valued |
| Indulgence (IND) | 44 | **Moderate** - Gratification controlled by social norms; restraint and duty emphasized |

**Source:** Hofstede et al. (2010). Empirical research published in *Cultures and Organizations*.

### How Dimensions Shape Spanish Culture

- **Moderate PDI:** Spain respects hierarchy but questions absolute authority. Family and organizational structures are hierarchical yet accessible.
- **Moderate IDV:** Balance between individual and group interests. While personal achievement matters, family loyalty and group harmony are equally important.
- **High UAI:** Strong preference for rules, planning, and structure. Spanish society values formal procedures, legal frameworks, and clear guidelines.
- **Moderate MAS:** Cooperation and relationships matter alongside achievement. Quality of life and work-life balance are valued alongside professional success.
- **Moderate LTO:** Mix of present-focused celebration and future planning. Spanish culture embraces spontaneity and enjoyment while maintaining practical sustainability.
- **Moderate IND:** Gratification is balanced with social duty. Restraint and self-discipline coexist with cultural celebration and enjoyment.

These dimensions inform the **[Position](culture_spanish_position.md)** (how Spain operates), **Pieces** (historical moments where dimensions collided), **Place** (Madrid's social patterns), and **Personas** (how individuals navigate these cultural pressures). Culture files carry standard Hofstede signal footers linking to this README; scoring is aggregate across all files, not per-file.

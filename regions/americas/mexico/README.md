## Persona Pair Architecture

**Opposing-pole pair.**

Rodrigo (PDI-high): the compadrazgo built him and he built it. Forty years of work through networks that are now expiring. The position taught him that nothing moves without someone who knows someone. It did not tell him that those someones have expiry dates.

Valentina (IDV-high): the individual trayectoria is real and she built it. The position taught her that personal path and collective loyalty do not contradict. It has not yet presented the bill the familia is accumulating. She defers it after each meeting. The account grows in silence.

Generation gap: culturally significant. Rodrigo's generation was shaped by the 1968 massacre and seventy-one years of PRI. Valentina's generation was shaped by NAFTA, digital platforms, and the possibility that the system might actually have other doors.

## How Dimensions Shape the Package

**PDI Very High:** Hierarchy is not contested; it is navigated. The patrón, the señor, the compadrazgo network -- all are load-bearing. The position file and the process file carry this most visibly. Rodrigo is the PDI-high persona anchor.

**IDV Low:** The familia is the first circle and the last safety net. Collective before individual, but not at the cost of the individual -- the tension between these two is what Valentina carries. The position file and the female persona carry this.

**UAI Very High:** The trámite is the process file's subject. Rules, protocols, and documents are real. So is the informal channel that moves them when they stall. Both registers are present because both are true.

**MAS High:** Ambición and esfuerzo are visible social values. The place file carries this in how the city rewards movement and hustle. The male persona carries it in what forty years of building looked like.

**LTO Low:** The present organizes everything. The Día de Muertos piece carries this most precisely: the ancestor is not historical, they are present, expected, received. The history file's Load Bearing section names this as a condition, not a choice.

**IND Very High:** The highest score in the project. The piece file (Día de Muertos) and the position file both carry this. The fiesta is evidence. The gozo is not incidental. The sacrificio lives alongside it without canceling it.

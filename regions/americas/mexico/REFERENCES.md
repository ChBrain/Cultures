## Source Registry

### Official and Institutional Sources
- Instituto Nacional de Antropología e Historia (INAH): inah.gob.mx -- archaeological and historical facts, museum holdings

- Constitución Política de los Estados Unidos Mexicanos, 1917 (current text): diputados.gob.mx
- INEGI (Instituto Nacional de Estadística y Geografía): inegi.org.mx -- demographic and geographic data

### Academic Sources
- Octavio Paz, *El laberinto de la soledad* (1950) -- cultural psychology of Mexican identity, mestizaje, and the wound of the Conquista
- Roger Bartra, *La jaula de la melancolía* (1987) -- the Mexican national character as cultural construction
- Claudio Lomnitz, *Death and the Idea of Mexico* (2005) -- Día de Muertos and Mexican relationship with death
- Alan Knight, *The Mexican Revolution* (1986) -- historical arc of the Revolution and its institutional legacy

### Contemporary and Cultural Sources

- Carlos Monsiváis, *Entrada libre: Crónicas de la sociedad que se organiza* (1987) -- the 1985 earthquake and civil society response

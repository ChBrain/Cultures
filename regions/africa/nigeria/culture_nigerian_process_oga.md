# Process: Oga

## Owner
- Project: Cultures
- Culture: Nigeria

## Initiated by
The [Nigerian](culture_nigerian_position.md) enters a room where resources, decisions, or access are held.
The oga is already in the room.
Not always as a person. Sometimes as a network node.
Sometimes as a title that points to another title that points to another.
The process begins without announcement.
Relationship is established before the matter is named.
The oga reads the approach before the words arrive.

## Direction
Toward the node that holds what is needed.
Not through procedure. Through person.
The formal channel exists. It is used when it serves.
When it does not serve, the circle routes around it.
The oga who can open the door is found through someone who knows someone.
Loyalty is the currency. Access is the return.

## Lever
The force: the oga provides protection, resources, and access to those beneath.
The circle beneath delivers loyalty, performance, and deference upward.
The resistance: the oga who accumulates without distributing loses the circle.
The node who cannot answer routes to the node above.
Accountability travels upward without arriving.
The lever holds both: without the oga's provision the circle has no anchor; without the circle's loyalty the oga has no mandate.
Neither side resolves. Both must remain active.
The oga who forgets his people is still called oga.
The title remains. The circle does not.

## Echo
What was granted is remembered.
What was withheld is also remembered.
The circle carries both without announcing either.
The next approach already knows what the last approach found.
The process does not close. It accumulates.
The loop opens again at Initiated by with that knowledge already in the room.

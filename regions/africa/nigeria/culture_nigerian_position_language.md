# Language: Nigerian English

## Owner
- Project: Cultures
- Culture: Nigeria

## Has
The formal register and the street register in the same mouth.
Nigerian English is not British English with distance.
It is English claimed, bent, and made to carry what English was not built to carry.
The institution speaks formal. The market speaks different.
Every educated Nigerian knows both and moves between them without announcement.

Authority arrives in full title.
People hear the rhythm before they hear the ask.
Engineer, Doctor, Chief, Alhaji, Barrister are heard before the matter is named.
The title is not courtesy. It is placement.
The room already knows.
It tells the room who holds what before a word of substance is spoken.
To skip the title is not informality. It is a statement.

The proverb enters without warning.
"When the music changes, so does the dance."
"Uchu onye n'eme ya mma" - what one does with care comes back with care.
"Ọmọ tí a kò kọ ni yóò ta ilé tì í" - the child we do not teach will sell the house that shelters us.
The proverb is not illustration. It is the argument, complete.
Whoever cannot follow it was not meant to.

Pidgin runs underneath.
Not broken. Not informal only.
A full register with its own grammar, its own weight, its own moment.
"No wahala." No trouble. Also: I will carry this. Also: we are still good.
"E don do." It is finished. The present closes. No extension requested.
Pidgin surfaces when the formal register cannot hold the feeling.
When it surfaces it does not translate itself.

## Orders
Greet first. Always.
The matter comes after the person is acknowledged.
To enter a room and begin with business is to begin without permission.

## Loses
The directness that has no relationship behind it.
Nigerian English cannot deliver a hard truth to a stranger without first building the ground.
Outside the Nigerian room, the title-before-substance reads as delay.
The proverb reads as evasion.
The "no wahala" reads as agreement when it was never agreement.
The language had not prepared for a room where warmth is not the precondition.

## Drives
On that loss:
finds the person in the room who can receive the message.
Routes through relationship, not around it.
The extra step is not inefficiency.
It is the condition under which the thing gets done at all.

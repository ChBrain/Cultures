# Persona: Emeka
## Title: Onye Ọrụ Ngo

## Owner
- Project: Cultures
- Culture: Nigeria

## Projection
Emeka bụ [nwoke](../../../engine/position_male.md) Igbo si [Naịjịrịa](culture_nigerian_position.md), na-arụ [ọrụ ngo](culture_nigerian_position_language_igbo.md) na [Eko](culture_nigerian_place_lagos.md).
A mụrụ ya na 1971 n'Onitsha. O wee bata Eko na 1993, otu afọ ka [Babangida kagburu June 12](culture_nigerian_piece_history.md), o ji otu kọntakt na akwụkwọ ndekọ.
Ọ naghị eburu akpa ọrụ. Ọ na-eburu ekwentị nwere SIM abụọ.
Ọ na-asụ [Bekee Naịjịrịa](culture_nigerian_position_language.md) na nzukọ, ma na-asụ Pidgin n'ebe onye na-anya ụgbọala nọ.
Ọ na-eche [ụzọ oga](culture_nigerian_process_oga.md) n'isi tupu ụlọ ọzụzụ atụgharị n'ebe ọ nọ.
N'ememe ịkpọ aha nwa, ọ na-eweta ite [jollof](culture_nigerian_piece_jollof.md) abụọ, ọ na-anọkwa ruo mgbe ọbịa ikpeazụ pụrụ.
Aha ya na-emechi okwu mgbe ọ kpọrọ ya. Mgbe e kpọrọ "Maazị Emeka," nwoke a na-aza n'ozu.

## Language
Asụsụ nne: Igbo. Asụsụ ọrụ: Bekee.
- Ikwu okwu: [Worn](../../../engine/process_speaking_worn.md). Bekee dabara ya dị ka uwe; Igbo na-apụta mgbe ụlọ bụ ezinụlọ.
- Ịnụ ihe: [Caught](../../../engine/process_hearing_caught.md). Ọ na-anụ ụlọ niile, gụnyere ọkwa a na-akọwaghị ya.
- Ịgụ akwụkwọ: [Followed](../../../engine/process_reading_followed.md). Nkwekọrịta, akwụkwọ ọrụ ndị gọọmenti, otu WhatsApp -- ọ na-eso ha niile.
- Ide ihe: [Drafted](../../../engine/process_writing_drafted.md). Akwụkwọ a na-ezigara ụlọ ọrụ gọọmenti, a na-ede ya ugboro abụọ tupu a ezipu ya.
N'oge ọdịda, [Asụsụ Nne](../../../engine/process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) na-apụta. Na nzukọ, ọ na-eburu [Bekee Azụmahịa](../../../engine/position_business_english.md) dị ka akwụkwọ njem nke ụlọ ahụ.

## Action
Nkwekọrịta nke ụlọ ọrụ gọọmenti akwụsịla. Ọnwa atọ. Oga nke nọ na desk ahụ akpaliteghị ya.
Emeka anaghị ezipu akwụkwọ ọzọ.
Ọ na-achọ onye gara ụlọ akwụkwọ ọzọ ya na nwanne nwoke nta oga ahụ.
Ọ na-akpọ onye ahụ. Ha na-ekwu maka ihe ndị ọzọ na mbụ.
Ka oge mkparịta ụka ahụ na-agwụ, e debere ụbọchị.
Nkwekọrịta na-akpụgharị n'ụbọchị ise.
Ọ na-ede ya n'okpuru: otu ihe si arụ.

## Shadow
Ọgbakọ ahụ kpụrụ ya. Ọ kpụrụ ọgbakọ ahụ.
Nkwekọrịta ọ bụla ọ rụtụrụ aka esitere n'aka onye maara onye.
Ọ na-agụ ụlọ ọ bụla maka mkpụrụ ahụ nke jikọrọ ọgbakọ ahụ.
Ọ na-achọta ya tupu nzukọ ekwue ya agwụcha.
Ihe ọ naghị ahụ: ụlọ ụfọdụ enweghị mkpụrụ ahụ.
Mkpebi ụfọdụ a na-eme site n'usoro, ma usoro ahụ bụ uko.
Nwa ya nwanyị gara mahadum na London nọ ugbu a n'ụlọ ebe ọgbakọ ọ kpụrụ ebe a anaghị eru.
Ọ na-akpọ ya kwa Sọnde. Ọ na-akọrọ ya ndị ọ maara na London.
Ọ na-ekele ya. Ọ naghị akpọ ndị ahụ.
Ọ na-ede ya n'okpuru: ọ ga-aghọta n'oge ọzọ.
Ọ nọ ugbu a na nghọta ọzọ.

## Tell
Mgbe nsogbu nke nọ n'ime ụlọ enweghị onye jikọrọ ya, ọ na-ewere ekwentị ya, na-elere ya anya, na-edobe ya, na-elee ya ọzọ.

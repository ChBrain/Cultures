# Persona: Hauwa
## Title: Shugabar Makaranta

## Owner
- Project: Cultures
- Culture: Nigeria

## Projection
Hauwa wata [mace](../../../engine/position_female.md) Bahaushiya ce daga [Najeriya](culture_nigerian_position.md), shugabar makarantar [islamiyya](culture_nigerian_position_language_hausa.md) ta 'yan mata a Sokoto, ba ta [Lagos](culture_nigerian_place_lagos.md) ba.
An haife ta a shekara ta 1968 a kauyen Wamakko, kafin fari da rashin shugabanci ya kawo arewa cikin matsanancin yanayi - [tarihin](culture_nigerian_piece_history.md) Sokoto ya rike abinda ta sani.
Ta yi karatun firamare a Boko, sannan ta yi sakandare a Government Girls' College Sokoto, kuma ta tafi Bayero University Kano don digiri a fannin koyarwa.
Ta auri Malam Sani, dan'uwa malamin ƙwararre. Suna da yara biyar; uku sun gama makaranta, biyu na karatu har yanzu.
Ta tashi kafin sallar asuba. Tana koyar da Quran sa'a kafin makaranta ta bude, sannan ta zama shugaba har yamma.
Tana magana da [Hausa](culture_nigerian_position_language.md) a gida, da iyali, da kasuwa, da malamai. Turanci tana amfani da shi ne kawai a wuraren da gwamnati ke bukatar takardun.
Tana karanta Ajami da Boko duka. Ana sanin mata a fadar Sarkin Musulmi - ba a matsayin baqo ba.
Idan akwai sabani a makaranta ko kuma matsalar gidan iyali a unguwar, sukan tafi wurinta kafin su tafi wurin [oga](culture_nigerian_process_oga.md) na karamar hukuma.
Lokacin biki, takan dafa [jollof](culture_nigerian_piece_jollof.md) na arewa - da masara, da kayan yaji, ba ƙwarai irin na Lagos ba.

## Language
Harshen uwa: Hausa. Harshen aiki: Turanci a takardun gwamnati kawai.
- Magana: [Worn](../../../engine/process_speaking_worn.md). Hausa ita ce tufafinta; Turanci tana sanya shi ne lokacin da masu duba makaranta suka zo.
- Ji: [Caught](../../../engine/process_hearing_caught.md). Tana jin dakin gabaki daya, har da abinda ba a fada ba.
- Karatu: [Followed](../../../engine/process_reading_followed.md). Quran, Ajami, dokokin gwamnati, kalandar makaranta - duk tana bin su a tsari daya.
- Rubutu: [Drafted](../../../engine/process_writing_drafted.md). Wasikar zuwa Ma'aikatar Ilimi tana rubuce ta sau biyu kafin a tura.
A lokacin tabarbarewa, [Harshen Uwa](../../../engine/process_speaking_mother_tongue.md) ke fitowa. A taron ma'aikatar ilimi, tana daukar [Turancin Aiki](../../../engine/position_business_english.md) a matsayin fasfo na dakin.

## Action
Iyaye uku sun zo wurinta. 'Ya'yansu mata sun ki zuwa makaranta tsawon mako biyu.
Hauwa ba ta yi tambaya akan dalilai a gabansu ba. Ta ce su shigo, sai ta shirya zaitun da goro.
Bayan gaisuwa, ta tambayi yara biyu daban-daban - ba mahaifansu ba.
Daya tana fargabar wani babban dan aji. Daya, mahaifiyarta tana son ta yi auren wani.
Hauwa ba ta kira hukumar 'yan sanda. Ba ta kira ma'aikatar yara.
Tana kira Hajiya Aisha, matar wani babba a fadar Sarki, wadda ta tafi makaranta da ita.
Hajiya Aisha tana kira mahaifin yarinyar farko. Sarki kansa yana aika sako ga mahaifiyar yarinyar ta biyu.
Yaran biyu sun dawo makaranta a ranar Litinin.
Ba ta gaya wa iyayen abinda ta yi.
Hauwa ta kira wannan "yadda ake yi."

## Shadow
A duk rayuwarta, hanyar gargajiya ta yi aiki.
Sarki yana wurin. Fadar tana wurin. Malamai na wurin. Iyalin nan na wurin.
Lokacin da matsala ta zo, ta san wa za ta kira, kuma ta san cewa za a karbi kiranta.
Abinda ba ta gani: cewa makarantar 'yarta ƙanwarta a Abuja, malamai a can ba su san sunan kowa ba.
Cewa idan 'yar ƙanwarta tana da matsala a ofishin gwamnati a Lagos, babu Hajiya Aisha da za ta kira.
'Yar ƙanwarta ta tafi ofishi sau goma. Ta kawo takardu duka. Babu wanda ya karba.
Hauwa tana cewa, "Ki yi hakuri, sai a samu hanya." Tana ba ta sunan wani malami da ke Kaduna, wanda ya san wani a Abuja.
'Yar ƙanwarta tana cewa, "Yaya." Amma ba ta kira malamin.
Tana zaune cikin tsari da bai dora kanta ba.
Hauwa ta gan ta a matsayin mai kasawa wajen tara hanya.
Ita kuwa tana ganin tsarin da bai shirya don ita ba.
Duka biyu sun fahimci nasu.

## Tell
Lokacin da matsalar ta zo a cikin dakin, tana karkadawa goro daya a hannunta sannan ta sa shi a bakinta kafin ta yi magana ta farko.

## Persona Pair Architecture

Opposing generation, partially opposing polarity.

**Wei Ming (male, 1963):** Anchor dimension PDI-high / LTO-high. Built the system from inside. Shadow: the system has become constitutive of him rather than the other way around. He cannot distinguish the consultation that found the real gap from the one that found nothing. He applies the same method to both.

**Priya (female, 1991):** Anchor dimension MAS-high with IDV tension. Shaped by the system, performing the collective so fluently that the performance is no longer distinguishable from belonging. Shadow: the cost is not visible in any standup. It shows up on a Sunday evening in an empty office.

Pair is opposing-pole on the IDV dimension (Wei Ming: full IDV-low, circle as infrastructure; Priya: IDV tension, the collective as performance that has started to cost). Shadow architectures are distinct: one is the system maintaining the person, the other is the performance that can no longer find its own seam.

## How Dimensions Shape the Package

**PDI -- High:** Hierarchy is present before the conversation begins. Title and seniority precede substance in the language file. The position file carries obedience, authority, and rank as structurally load-bearing. Wei Ming's persona is anchored here: the chain of authority is the frame through which he reads every proposal.

**IDV -- Low:** The circle is the primary infrastructure. The position file, the kiasu process, and both personas all carry loyalty, kinship, and in-group as the operating substrate beneath the institution. The hawker centre is the collective in its only non-performed form.

**UAI -- Very Low:** Governance instrumentality is the dominant register. The kiasu process is not anxiety -- it is calibration. The language file carries flexibility and adaptability without framing them as virtues. Rules exist and are followed, but their authority is instrumental.

**MAS -- Moderate:** Achievement and excellence run through every file, anchored in the meritocracy narrative and the kiasu process. Care and welfare appear in the hawker centre and in the community registers of both personas. Neither side overwhelms the other.

**LTO -- High:** Perseverance and foresight are embedded in the history file (every entry is a long-term calculation), the position file (continuity, prudence, planning), and Wei Ming's persona (the process that holds because the consultation incorporated what the data missed).

**IND -- Moderate:** Restraint and composure in public-facing registers. The hawker centre and the place file both carry the indulgent pole in its designated civic form. Priya's shadow shows the cost of sustained public restraint when the private register has nowhere to go.

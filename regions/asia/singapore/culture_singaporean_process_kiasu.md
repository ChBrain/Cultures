# Process: Kiasu

## Owner
- Project: Cultures
- Culture: Singapore

## Initiated by
The [Singaporean](culture_singaporean_position.md) enters a situation where the resource is finite and the competition is real.
A place in the school. A flat in the estate. A seat at the table.
The [history](culture_singaporean_piece_history.md) is already in the room: as the knowledge that the island is small, the margin is thin, and what was not secured yesterday may not be available tomorrow.
Not as anxiety. As calibration.
The process begins before the queue forms.
Information is gathered in advance. The position in the line is taken before the line is announced.
To arrive when everyone else arrives is to have already lost ground.

## Direction
Toward not losing what can still be kept.
Not toward winning.
Toward the position that cannot be taken from you after the room closes.
Toward the qualification that outlasts the economic cycle.
Toward the flat, the school, the connection that will still be there when you must improvise and the rules move underfoot.

## Lever
The force: the knowledge, carried in the body since the first examination, that preparation determines outcome.
Planning is not anxiety management. It is correct behaviour in a place with no margin - calibrated, flexible, ready to adapt when the ground shifts.
The method stays experimental in small moves, tolerant of ambiguity and risk, agile enough not to lose the line.
The resistance: the cost of running the calculation every time.
The vigilance that kiasu requires does not switch off between situations.
It recalibrates for the coffee shop queue with the same mechanism it uses for the school registration.
The lever holds both: without the preparation the position is lost to someone who arrived ready; without the recognition that the calculation has a cost, the preparation becomes indistinguishable from the fear it was built to prevent.

## Echo
The position secured feeds the next preparation.
The school secured feeds the next examination.
The flat secured feeds the next investment.
What was not secured is noted. The gap between what was achieved and what was available is the data that drives the next cycle.
The process does not declare victory. It restarts.
The light closes here and opens again at Initiated by.
